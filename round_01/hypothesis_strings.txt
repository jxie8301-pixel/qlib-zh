hypothesis
I propose creating three simple but effective factors: 1) 10-day price momentum, 2) 20-day volatility, and 3) Volume-Weighted Average Price (VWAP). These factors capture different aspects of market behavior - momentum, risk, and average trading price - and are known to have predictive power in financial markets.
reason
These factors are chosen for their simplicity, interpretability, and established effectiveness in quantitative finance. Price momentum captures trending behavior, volatility measures risk, and VWAP provides a volume-adjusted price reference. They're computationally efficient to calculate and provide diverse signals that can complement each other in a factor model.
